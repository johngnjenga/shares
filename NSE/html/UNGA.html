UNGA share price on Nairobi Securities Exchange

Unga Group Limited is a Kenya-based holding company. The Company has two business segments, namely Consumer, and Animal Nutrition and Health. The Consumer segment produces products for human consumption. The Animal Nutrition and Health segment produces animal feed and mineral supplement products, and distributes products for animal health. The Company, through its subsidiaries, is engaged in the milling of wheat and maize, and the manufacture of packaging materials and animal nutrition products. The Company's wholly owned subsidiary is Unga Investments Limited, which has a 65% holding in its subsidiary, Unga Holding Limited. As of June 2010, the Company's major shareholders were Victus Limited and Moses Thara, among others.

Unga Group Limited is listed on the Nairobi Securities Exchange (NSE). Unga is traded on the NSE under the ticker symbol “UNGA”. The International Securities Identification Number (ISIN) of NSE:UNGA is KE0000000497. Unga Group Limited is currently the 44th most valuable stock on the NSE with a market capitalization of KES 1.82 billion, which is about 0.054% of the Nairobi Securities Exchange equity market.
