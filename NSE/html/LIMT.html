LIMT - Limuru Tea Company Limited

Feb 16, 2026 at 07:23 Twitter · Facebook
LIMT share price on Nairobi Securities Exchange

The Limuru Tea Company Limited is a Kenyan company engaged in the growing of green leaf tea. The Company owns 275 hectares of tea land situated four kilometers to the east of Limuru Town. The Limuru Tea Company Limited is an outgrower to Unilever Tea Kenya Limited. Unilever Tea Kenya acts as the Company's managing agent in the growing, manufacturing, sales and marketing of its teas. The tea estate green leaf is manufactured in the Unilever Tea Kenya Mabroukie factory from where it is sold mainly for export.

Limuru Tea Company Limited is listed on the Nairobi Securities Exchange (NSE). Limuru Tea is traded on the NSE under the ticker symbol “LIMT”. The International Securities Identification Number (ISIN) of NSE:LIMT is KE0000000356. Limuru Tea Company Limited is currently the 47th most valuable stock on the NSE with a market capitalization of KES 1.26 billion, which is about 0.037% of the Nairobi Securities Exchange equity market.
