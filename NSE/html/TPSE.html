TPS Eastern Africa Limited is a Kenya-based company, which owns and operates hotel and lodge facilities in Eastern Africa, serving the business and tourist markets. The Company, along with its subsidiaries, is principally engaged in owning and operating 32 resorts, safari lodges and hotels, which are located in East Africa (Kenya, Tanzania, Rwanda, Uganda and Mozambique) and Southern Asia (Pakistan, Afghanistan and Tajikistan). Its segments include Kenya Hotels, Kenya Lodges and Tanzania Lodges. The Company operates through several subsidiaries, including Tourism Promotion Services (Kenya) Limited, Tourism Promotion Services (Tanzania) Limited & Tourism Promotion Services (Zanzibar) Limited.

TPS Eastern Africa Serena Limited is listed on the Nairobi Securities Exchange (NSE). TPS Eastern Africa Serena is traded on the NSE under the ticker symbol “TPSE”. The International Securities Identification Number (ISIN) of NSE:TPSE is KE0000000539. TPS Eastern Africa Serena Limited is currently the 37th most valuable stock on the NSE with a market capitalization of KES 3.09 billion, which is about 0.092% of the Nairobi Securities Exchange equity market.
