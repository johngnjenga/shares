Kapchorua Tea Company Limited is a Kenyan company engaged in the cultivation, manufacture and sale of tea. As of March 31, 2002, the Company's mature planted area was 661.75 hectares and immature planted area was 23 hectares. During the fiscal year ended March 31, 2002, Kapchorua Tea Company Limited's tea production was 3,465 kilograms, of which was 1,789 kilograms was from its own estate and 1,676 kilograms was bought leaf.

Kapchorua Tea Company Limited is listed on the Nairobi Securities Exchange (NSE). Kapchorua Tea is traded on the NSE under the ticker symbol “KAPC”. The International Securities Identification Number (ISIN) of NSE:KAPC is KE4000001760. Kapchorua Tea Company Limited is currently the 44th most valuable stock on the NSE with a market capitalization of KES 1.95 billion, which makes about 0.058% of the Nairobi Securities Exchange equity market.
