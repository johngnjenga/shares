British American Tobacco (Kenya) Limited is a Kenya-based company. The Company is engaged in the manufacture and sale of cigarettes and tobacco. The Company manufactures and sells cigarettes to distributors and fellow subsidiaries of the ultimate parent company. Its parent company is British American Tobacco Plc.

British American Tobacco Kenya is listed on the Nairobi Securities Exchange (NSE). British American Tobacco is traded on the NSE under the ticker symbol “BAT”. The International Securities Identification Number (ISIN) of NSE:BAT is KE0000000075. British American Tobacco Kenya is currently the 12th most valuable stock on the NSE with a market capitalization of KES 49.9 billion, which makes about 1.49% of the Nairobi Securities Exchange equity market.
