Crown Paints Kenya Limited is a Kenya-based paint manufacturing company. The company, together with its subsidiaries, is engaged in the manufacture and sale of paints, adhesives, decorating sundries, polyvinyl acetate (PVA) emulsion, alkyd resins and tins. The Company's products include decorative, automotive and industrial paints; intermediate products, such as primers and undercoats, among others; road marking paints; thinners, and adhesives. The Company is active on the Kenya, Uganda, Burundi, Southern Sudan, Congo and Rwanda markets. Crown Paints Kenya Ltd's subsidiaries comprise the wholly owned Crown Berger Allied Industries Limited and Crown Buildings and Products Limited (Uganda).

Crown Paints Kenya Limited is listed on the Nairobi Securities Exchange (NSE). Crown Paints is traded on the NSE under the ticker symbol “CRWN”. The International Securities Identification Number (ISIN) of NSE:CRWN is KE0000000141. Crown Paints Kenya Limited is currently the 25th most valuable stock on the NSE with a market capitalization of KES 8.36 billion, which makes about 0.255% of the Nairobi Securities Exchange equity market.
